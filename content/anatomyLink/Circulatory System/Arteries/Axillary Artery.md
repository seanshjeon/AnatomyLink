# Axillary Artery

> The axillary artery is the **main artery of the axilla** and upper arm, continuous with the [Subclavian Artery](./anatomyLink/Circulatory-System/Arteries/Subclavian-Artery.md) at the lateral border of the first rib and continuing as the [Brachial Artery](./anatomyLink/Circulatory-System/Arteries/Brachial-Artery.md) at the inferior border of teres major. It is the principal blood supply to the shoulder and upper limb.

## 🔗 Key Connections
- [Circulatory System](./anatomyLink/Circulatory-System/Circulatory-System.md)
- Proximal: [Subclavian Artery](./anatomyLink/Circulatory-System/Arteries/Subclavian-Artery.md) (at lateral border of 1st rib)
- Distal: [Brachial Artery](./anatomyLink/Circulatory-System/Arteries/Brachial-Artery.md) (at inferior border of teres major)
- Surrounded by: [Brachial Plexus](./anatomyLink/Nervous-System/Peripheral-Nervous-System/Brachial-Plexus.md) cords (medial, lateral, posterior)
- Relations: Pectoralis minor divides axillary artery into 3 parts

## 🔬 Three Parts (relative to Pectoralis Minor)

| Part | Location | Branches | Mnemonic |
|------|----------|----------|---------|
| **1st** (medial to PM) | Medial to pectoralis minor | 1 branch: Superior thoracic artery | **1 branch** |
| **2nd** (behind PM) | Behind pectoralis minor | 2 branches: Thoracoacromial + Lateral thoracic | **2 branches** |
| **3rd** (lateral to PM) | Lateral to pectoralis minor | 3 branches: Subscapular + Anterior/Posterior circumflex humeral | **3 branches** |

## ⚙️ Key Branches
- **Subscapular artery** (largest) → circumflex scapular + thoracodorsal
- **Posterior circumflex humeral artery** → with axillary nerve through quadrilateral space around surgical neck of [Humerus](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Upper-Limb/Humerus.md)
- **Anterior circumflex humeral artery** → around surgical neck; supplies humeral head

## 🏥 Clinical Significance
- **Axillary artery injury** — shoulder dislocation (anterior) → neurovascular bundle at risk; check radial pulse + axillary nerve (deltoid sensory, regimental badge area)
- **Surgical neck of humerus fracture** — anterior + posterior circumflex humeral arteries at risk → avascular necrosis of humeral head
- **Brachial plexus compression** — cords of [Brachial Plexus](./anatomyLink/Nervous-System/Peripheral-Nervous-System/Brachial-Plexus.md) surround axillary artery; trauma → combined vascular + nerve injury
