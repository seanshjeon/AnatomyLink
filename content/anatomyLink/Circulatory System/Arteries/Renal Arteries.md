# Renal Arteries

> The renal arteries are paired **lateral branches of the abdominal [Aorta](./anatomyLink/Circulatory System/Arteries/Aorta.md)** at L1–L2, supplying the kidneys. The **right renal artery is longer**, crossing posterior to the inferior vena cava ([Inferior Vena Cava](./anatomyLink/Circulatory System/Veins/Inferior Vena Cava.md)).

## 🔗 Key Connections
- Arise from: [Aorta](./anatomyLink/Circulatory System/Arteries/Aorta.md) (L1–L2, lateral branches)
- Supply: kidneys (each kidney receives 20–25% of resting cardiac output)
- Right renal artery passes: posterior to [Inferior Vena Cava](./anatomyLink/Circulatory System/Veins/Inferior Vena Cava.md)

## 🗂️ Key Information

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Origin | [Aorta](./anatomyLink/Circulatory System/Arteries/Aorta.md) at L1–L2 level |
| Right renal artery | Longer; passes posterior to IVC, right renal vein, head of pancreas, descending duodenum |
| Left renal artery | Shorter; passes posterior to left renal vein, body/tail of pancreas |
| Branches | Segmental arteries (5 per kidney) → lobar → interlobar → arcuate → interlobular → afferent arterioles |
| Venous drainage | Right renal vein → [Inferior Vena Cava](./anatomyLink/Circulatory System/Veins/Inferior Vena Cava.md) directly; Left renal vein → crosses anterior to [Aorta](./anatomyLink/Circulatory System/Arteries/Aorta.md) → IVC |

## 🔑 Renal Blood Flow
Each kidney receives ~400–500 mL/min (combined ~1000 mL/min = 20% of cardiac output). End-arteries — no significant collateral circulation → infarction with occlusion.

## 🏥 Clinical Significance
- **Renal artery stenosis (RAS)** — most commonly atherosclerotic (ostial); causes renovascular hypertension (↑ renin → ↑ angiotensin II); fibromuscular dysplasia in young women (mid-artery, "string of beads" appearance)
- **Renal artery aneurysm** — usually at bifurcation; rupture risk in pregnancy
- **Renal infarction** — thromboembolic; sudden flank pain + haematuria; wedge-shaped infarct on CT
- **Nutcracker syndrome** — left renal vein compressed between [Aorta](./anatomyLink/Circulatory System/Arteries/Aorta.md) and SMA → haematuria, left flank pain, varicocele (left)
- **Transplant renal artery** — anastomosed to external iliac artery; transplant placed in right iliac fossa
