# Internal Iliac Artery

> The internal iliac artery (hypogastric artery) is the **main arterial supply to the pelvis**, branching from the common [Iliac Arteries](./anatomyLink/Circulatory-System/Arteries/Iliac-Arteries.md) at the pelvic brim (L5/S1 level). It supplies pelvic viscera, perineum, gluteal region, and medial thigh.

## 🔗 Key Connections
- [Circulatory System](./anatomyLink/Circulatory-System/Circulatory-System.md)
- Origin: Common iliac artery bifurcation at L5/S1 (pelvic brim)
- Related: [Sacral Plexus](./anatomyLink/Nervous-System/Peripheral-Nervous-System/Sacral-Plexus.md) (posterior), [Ureter](./anatomyLink/Urinary-System/Ureter.md) (crosses anteriorly at pelvic brim), [Reproductive System](./anatomyLink/Reproductive-System/Reproductive-System.md) pelvic organs
- Divides into: **Anterior division** (visceral) + **Posterior division** (parietal)

## 🔬 Branches

### Anterior Division — Visceral
| Branch | Supplies |
|--------|---------|
| Superior vesical artery | [Urinary Bladder](./anatomyLink/Urinary-System/Urinary-Bladder.md) (superior), [Ureter](./anatomyLink/Urinary-System/Ureter.md), [Vas Deferens](./anatomyLink/Reproductive-System/Male/Vas-Deferens.md) |
| Inferior vesical artery (male) / vaginal artery (female) | Bladder base, [Prostate Gland](./anatomyLink/Reproductive-System/Male/Prostate-Gland.md) / [Vagina](./anatomyLink/Reproductive-System/Female/Vagina.md), lower [Uterus](./anatomyLink/Reproductive-System/Female/Uterus.md) |
| **Uterine artery** (female) | [Uterus](./anatomyLink/Reproductive-System/Female/Uterus.md), [Cervix](./anatomyLink/Reproductive-System/Female/Cervix.md), [Uterine Tubes](./anatomyLink/Reproductive-System/Female/Uterine-Tubes.md), [Ovaries](./anatomyLink/Reproductive-System/Female/Ovaries.md) |
| Middle rectal artery | Rectum |
| Internal pudendal artery | Perineum, external genitalia ([Penis](./anatomyLink/Reproductive-System/Male/Penis.md), [Vulva](./anatomyLink/Reproductive-System/Female/Vulva.md)), anal canal |
| Obturator artery | Medial thigh (obturator canal) |
| Inferior gluteal artery | Gluteal region (with sciatic nerve) |

### Posterior Division — Parietal
| Branch | Supplies |
|--------|---------|
| Iliolumbar artery | Iliacus, psoas, quadratus lumborum |
| Lateral sacral arteries | [Sacrum](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Spine/Sacrum.md), sacral canal |
| Superior gluteal artery | Gluteal muscles (largest branch) |

## 🏥 Clinical Significance
- **Uterine artery embolisation (UAE)** — treatment for fibroids; catheter → uterine artery → embolise
- **PPH (postpartum haemorrhage)** — internal iliac artery ligation as last resort for haemostasis
- **Pelvic trauma** — internal iliac branches at high risk; major haemorrhage
- **Internal pudendal nerve** — travels with internal pudendal artery through Alcock's canal; pudendal nerve block
