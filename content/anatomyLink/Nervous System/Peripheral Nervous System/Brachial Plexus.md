# Brachial Plexus

> The brachial plexus is the **network of nerve roots C5–T1** that forms all the major nerves of the upper limb. It is organised into roots → trunks → divisions → cords → terminal branches.

## 🔗 Key Connections
- Root values: C5–T1 (from [Spinal Cord](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Central Nervous System/Spinal Cord.md))
- Formed between: anterior and middle scalene muscles (in the neck)
- Passes through: posterior triangle of neck → beneath [Clavicle](./anatomyLink/Skeletal System/Upper Limb/Clavicle.md) → axilla
- Terminal branches: [Axillary Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Axillary Nerve.md), [Radial Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Radial Nerve.md), [Median Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Median Nerve.md), [Ulnar Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Ulnar Nerve.md), [Musculocutaneous Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Musculocutaneous Nerve.md)
- Relation to: [Subclavian Artery](./anatomyLink/Circulatory System/Arteries/Subclavian Artery.md) (runs superior), [Humerus](./anatomyLink/Skeletal System/Upper Limb/Humerus.md) (surgical neck — axillary nerve)

## 🗂️ Organisation (Roots → Trunks → Divisions → Cords → Branches)

### Trunks (in posterior triangle of neck)
| Trunk | Roots | Level |
|-------|-------|-------|
| **Upper trunk** | C5 + C6 | Above clavicle |
| **Middle trunk** | C7 | Above clavicle |
| **Lower trunk** | C8 + T1 | Above clavicle |

### Cords (named by relation to axillary artery)
| Cord | From | Key Branches |
|------|------|--------------|
| **Lateral cord** | Anterior divisions of upper + middle trunks | [Musculocutaneous Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Musculocutaneous Nerve.md), lateral root of [Median Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Median Nerve.md) |
| **Medial cord** | Anterior division of lower trunk | [Ulnar Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Ulnar Nerve.md), medial root of [Median Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Median Nerve.md), medial cutaneous nerves |
| **Posterior cord** | All posterior divisions | [Axillary Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Axillary Nerve.md), [Radial Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Radial Nerve.md) |

### Terminal Branches (the 5 main nerves)
| Nerve | Cord | Roots | Key Supply |
|-------|------|-------|------------|
| [Musculocutaneous Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Musculocutaneous Nerve.md) | Lateral | C5–C6 | [Biceps Brachii](./anatomyLink/Muscular System/Upper Limb/Biceps Brachii.md), brachialis; lateral forearm sensation |
| [Axillary Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Axillary Nerve.md) | Posterior | C5–C6 | [Deltoid](./anatomyLink/Muscular System/Upper Limb/Deltoid.md), teres minor ([Rotator Cuff](./anatomyLink/Muscular System/Upper Limb/Rotator Cuff.md)); regimental badge sensation |
| [Radial Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Radial Nerve.md) | Posterior | C5–C8, T1 | All extensors of arm + forearm; dorsal hand sensation |
| [Median Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Median Nerve.md) | Lateral + Medial | C6–T1 | Anterior forearm flexors; thenar muscles; lateral 3.5 digits |
| [Ulnar Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous System/Upper Limb Nerves/Ulnar Nerve.md) | Medial | C8–T1 | FCU, FDP (medial); intrinsics; medial 1.5 digits |

### Pre-terminal Branches (arising before the cords)
| Nerve | Root | Supply |
|-------|------|--------|
| Dorsal scapular | C5 | Rhomboids, levator scapulae |
| Long thoracic | C5–C7 | Serratus anterior (winging of [Scapula](./anatomyLink/Skeletal System/Upper Limb/Scapula.md) if damaged) |
| Suprascapular | C5–C6 | Supraspinatus + infraspinatus ([Rotator Cuff](./anatomyLink/Muscular System/Upper Limb/Rotator Cuff.md)) |
| Nerve to subclavius | C5–C6 | Subclavius |
| Medial + lateral pectoral | C5–T1 | [Pectoralis Major](./anatomyLink/Muscular System/Trunk/Pectoralis Major.md) + pectoralis minor |
| Upper + lower subscapular | C5–C6 | Subscapularis ([Rotator Cuff](./anatomyLink/Muscular System/Upper Limb/Rotator Cuff.md)), teres major |
| Thoracodorsal | C6–C8 | [Latissimus Dorsi](./anatomyLink/Muscular System/Trunk/Latissimus Dorsi.md) |

## 🏥 Clinical Significance
- **Erb's palsy (upper trunk — C5, C6)** — most common; birth injury (shoulder dystocia) or motorcycle fall; "waiter's tip" posture (arm adducted + internally rotated, elbow extended, wrist pronated + flexed); loss of shoulder abduction, elbow flexion, forearm supination
- **Klumpke's palsy (lower trunk — C8, T1)** — traction injury with arm forced upward; "claw hand" (intrinsic minus); thenar + hypothenar wasting; Horner's syndrome (T1 sympathetic fibres)
- **Thoracic outlet syndrome (TOS)** — compression between clavicle + 1st rib or by cervical rib; neurogenic TOS (most common): medial forearm + hand pain + weakness; vascular TOS: [Subclavian Artery](./anatomyLink/Circulatory System/Arteries/Subclavian Artery.md) or vein compression
- **Winged scapula** — long thoracic nerve (C5–C7) injury → serratus anterior paralysis → medial [Scapula](./anatomyLink/Skeletal System/Upper Limb/Scapula.md) border wings out on wall push-up
- **Brachial plexus birth injury (BPBI)** — overall incidence ~1–3/1000 live births
