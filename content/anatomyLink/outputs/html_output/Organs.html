🍽️ Digestive Organs

Organ Key Function
Oral Cavity Mastication, salivary digestion, swallowing initiation
Esophagus Muscular conduit; peristalsis carries food to stomach
Stomach Reservoir; HCl + pepsinogen → protein digestion
Small Intestine Primary site of nutrient absorption (~6 m; duodenum, jejunum, ileum)
Large Intestine Water + electrolyte absorption; faeces formation
Rectum and Anal Canal Faecal storage + controlled defecation
Liver Bile production, detoxification, protein synthesis, metabolism
Gallbladder Bile storage + concentration; releases bile on fatty meals
Pancreas Exocrine: digestive enzymes; Endocrine: insulin + glucagon

🫀 Other Abdominal / Retroperitoneal Organs

Organ Key Function
Kidneys Filtration, fluid + electrolyte balance, erythropoietin; see Renal Arteries
Spleen Immune filtration, red blood cell destruction, blood reservoir
Adrenal glands Cortisol, aldosterone (cortex); adrenaline (medulla)
Urinary bladder Urine storage + micturition
