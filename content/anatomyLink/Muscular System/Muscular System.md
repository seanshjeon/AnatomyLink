# Muscular System

> The human body has over **600 skeletal muscles**. Muscles work by attaching to bones via tendons — contracting to produce movement at joints.

## 📌 Key Concepts
- **Origin** — The fixed, proximal attachment point of a muscle
- **Insertion** — The movable, distal attachment point
- **Action** — The movement produced by contraction
- **Innervation** — The nerve supplying the muscle
- **Agonist / Antagonist** — Muscles working together or in opposition
