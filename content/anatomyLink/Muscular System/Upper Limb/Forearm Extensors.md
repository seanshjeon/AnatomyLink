# Forearm Extensors

> The forearm extensors occupy the **posterior (extensor) compartment** of the forearm. Most arise from the **lateral epicondyle** of the [Humerus](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Upper-Limb/Humerus.md) (common extensor origin) and are innervated by the **[Radial Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous-System/Upper-Limb-Nerves/Radial-Nerve.md)** (via the posterior interosseous nerve — PIN).

## 🔗 Key Connections
- [Muscular System](./anatomyLink/Muscular-System/Muscular-System.md)
- Innervation: **[Radial Nerve](./anatomyLink/Nervous-System/Upper-Limb-Nerves/Radial-Nerve.md)** — superficial branch (ECRL, ECRB) and **posterior interosseous nerve (PIN)** (all others)
- Bony landmarks: [Lateral Epicondyle](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Upper-Limb/Lateral-Epicondyle.md) ([Humerus](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Upper-Limb/Humerus.md)), [Radius](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Upper-Limb/Radius.md), [Ulna](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Upper-Limb/Ulna.md)

## 🔬 Superficial Layer (Common Extensor Origin — Lateral Epicondyle)

| Muscle | Insertion | Action | Nerve |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Extensor Carpi Radialis Longus (ECRL)** | Base of 2nd metacarpal | Wrist extension + radial deviation | Radial N. (C6–C7) |
| **Extensor Carpi Radialis Brevis (ECRB)** | Base of 3rd metacarpal | Wrist extension | Radial N. (C6–C7) |
| **Extensor Digitorum (ED)** | Extensor hoods of fingers 2–5 | Extends fingers + wrist | PIN (C7–C8) |
| **Extensor Digiti Minimi (EDM)** | Extensor hood of 5th finger | Extends little finger | PIN (C7–C8) |
| **Extensor Carpi Ulnaris (ECU)** | Base of 5th metacarpal | Wrist extension + ulnar deviation | PIN (C7–C8) |

## 🔬 Deep Layer

| Muscle | Insertion | Action | Nerve |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------|
| Supinator | Lateral [Radius](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Upper-Limb/Radius.md) | **Supination** | PIN (C6) |
| Abductor Pollicis Longus (APL) | Base of 1st metacarpal | Abducts thumb | PIN (C7–C8) |
| Extensor Pollicis Brevis (EPB) | Base of proximal phalanx of thumb | Extends MCP of thumb | PIN (C7–C8) |
| Extensor Pollicis Longus (EPL) | Base of distal phalanx of thumb | Extends IP of thumb | PIN (C7–C8) |
| Extensor Indicis (EI) | Extensor hood of index | Extends index finger | PIN (C7–C8) |

**Anatomical snuffbox:** Bounded by EPL (medially), APL + EPB (laterally); floor = scaphoid + [Radial Artery](./anatomyLink/Circulatory-System/Arteries/Radial-Artery.md); tenderness → scaphoid fracture

## 🏥 Clinical Significance
- **Lateral epicondylitis (Tennis elbow)** — overuse of ECRB origin; lateral elbow pain; Cozen's test
- **PIN palsy (posterior interosseous nerve)** — finger drop (cannot extend MCP); wrist deviation to radial side (ECRL/B spared); causes: radial head dislocation, compression in arcade of Frohse (supinator)
- **De Quervain's tenosynovitis** — APL + EPB in 1st dorsal compartment; radial wrist pain; Finkelstein test positive
- **Radial nerve injury** — wrist drop (all extensors lost)
