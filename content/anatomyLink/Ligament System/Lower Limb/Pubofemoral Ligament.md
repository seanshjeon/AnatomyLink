# Pubofemoral Ligament

> The **pubofemoral ligament** reinforces the inferior and anterior hip capsule, arising from the pubic part of the acetabular rim and blending with the [[Iliofemoral Ligament]]. It limits **abduction and extension**.

## 🔗 Key Connections
- **Origin** — Iliopubic eminence + superior pubic ramus ([[Hip Bone]])
- **Insertion** — Anterior intertrochanteric fossa; blends with [[Iliofemoral Ligament]]
- **Limits** — Abduction + extension + lateral rotation
- **Related ligaments** — [[Iliofemoral Ligament]], [[Ischiofemoral Ligament]]

## 🏥 Clinical Significance
- **Hip dislocation** — Inferior dislocation involves pubofemoral + iliofemoral rupture
- **Hip replacement (THA)** — Anterior approach must release or work around pubofemoral region
- **Hip labral tears** — Inferior labral tears in region of pubofemoral attachment
