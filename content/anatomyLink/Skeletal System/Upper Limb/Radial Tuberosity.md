# Radial Tuberosity

> The **radial tuberosity** (bicipital tuberosity) is a roughened oval prominence just distal to the radial head on the anteromedial [Radius](./anatomyLink/Skeletal System/Upper Limb/Radius.md), where the [Biceps Brachii](./anatomyLink/Muscular System/Upper Limb/Biceps Brachii.md) tendon inserts.

## 🔗 Key Connections
- [Radius](./anatomyLink/Skeletal System/Upper Limb/Radius.md) — proximal radius, distal to the radial head
- [Biceps Brachii](./anatomyLink/Muscular System/Upper Limb/Biceps Brachii.md) — inserts here via the biceps tendon (flexion + supination)

## 🏥 Clinical Significance
- **Distal biceps tendon rupture** — Avulsion at radial tuberosity; "popeye" deformity, weak supination
- **Surgical repair** — Reattachment to radial tuberosity restores supination strength
