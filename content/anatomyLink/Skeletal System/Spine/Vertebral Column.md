# Vertebral Column

> The vertebral column consists of **26 bones** forming the central axis of the body. It protects the spinal cord, supports body weight, and connects the skull to the pelvis.

## 🔗 Connected Structures
- [Skull](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Skull-Region/Skull.md) — Atlas (C1) articulates with the occipital bone at the **atlanto-occipital joint**
- [Ribs](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Thorax/Ribs.md) — Thoracic vertebrae (T1–T12) articulate with ribs at **costovertebral joints**
- [Sternum](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Thorax/Sternum.md) — Indirectly connected via the ribs
- [Hip Bone](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Lower-Limb/Hip-Bone.md) — Sacrum articulates with the ilium at the **sacroiliac joint (SIJ)**

## 🔗 Associated Muscles
- [Erector Spinae](./anatomyLink/Muscular-System/Trunk/Erector-Spinae.md) — Primary extensors running along the entire spine
- [Trapezius](./anatomyLink/Muscular-System/Trunk/Trapezius.md) — Attaches to spinous processes of cervical and thoracic vertebrae
- [Latissimus Dorsi](./anatomyLink/Muscular-System/Trunk/Latissimus-Dorsi.md) — Originates from lower thoracic and lumbar spinous processes
- [Iliopsoas](./anatomyLink/Muscular-System/Lower-Limb/Iliopsoas.md) — Psoas major originates from lumbar vertebral bodies

## 📐 Spinal Curvatures

| Curvature | Direction | Type |
|-----------|-----------|------|
| Cervical lordosis | Anterior convexity | Secondary |
| Thoracic kyphosis | Posterior convexity | Primary |
| Lumbar lordosis | Anterior convexity | Secondary |
| Sacral kyphosis | Posterior convexity | Primary |

> **Primary curves** = present from fetal life / **Secondary curves** = develop postnatally

## 🔑 [Intervertebral Discs](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Spine/Intervertebral-Disc.md)
- Located between C2/C3 and L5/S1 (23 discs total)
- **Annulus fibrosus** (outer fibrocartilage ring) + **Nucleus pulposus** (inner hydrated gel)
- Absorb approximately **25%** of axial load

## 🔑 Key Vertebral Landmarks
- [Spinous Process](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Spine/Spinous-Process.md) — Posterior bony projections; palpable midline; ligament/muscle attachments
- [Transverse Process](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Spine/Transverse-Process.md) — Lateral projections; rib articulation (thoracic); muscle attachments
- [Intervertebral Disc](./anatomyLink/Skeletal-System/Spine/Intervertebral-Disc.md) — Fibrocartilage shock absorbers between vertebral bodies (23 total)

## 🏥 Clinical Significance
- **Disc herniation (HIVD)** — Most common at L4/L5 and L5/S1
- **Scoliosis** — Lateral curvature >10° in the coronal plane
- **Spinal stenosis** — Most common in the lumbar region
- **Spinal cord injury** — Level of injury determines extent of paralysis
