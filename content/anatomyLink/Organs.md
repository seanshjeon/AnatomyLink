## 🫁 Respiratory Organs

| [Gallbladder](./anatomyLink/Digestive System/Accessory Organs/Gallbladder.md) | Bile storage + concentration; releases bile on fatty meals |
| [Pancreas](./anatomyLink/Digestive System/Accessory Organs/Pancreas.md) | Exocrine: digestive enzymes; Endocrine: insulin + glucagon |
